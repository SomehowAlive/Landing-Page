It's Autumn, The year's last loveliest smile

Dreaming of pumpkin lattes, caramel apples, turkey, and mashed potatoes? So are we! It's finally Autumn, a favorite season to many who enjoy the fun foods, festivities, and cooler weather, the 'Season of mists and mellow fruitfulness', autumn is a season famous for its harvest times, turning leaves, cooling temperatures and darkening nights.

What's Autumn Known For ?

One of the most stunning signs of autumn is the turning of the leaves. The shorter days are a sign to trees to begin to prepare for winter.

Chlorophyll is the chemical which makes tree leaves green and as it declines other chemicals become more prominent in the leaves.

The full moon in the fall that occurs during the equinox is much brighter (almost orange) and rises much earlier than a typical full moon.

According to research children born in the fall may be more likely to live longer and up to 100 years and it's not pseudoscience.
